These pages are used and maintained by ArcLight engineers to provide project information to ArcLight engineers and other interested parties. The purpose of these pages is to provide a familiar simple, consistent, and version controlled mechanism for publishing project standards, policies and engineering information.

ArcLight technology provides mobile vehicles with two-way broadband Internet access via satellite communications. ArcLight consists of one or more ArcLight Hubs and many ViaSat Mobile Terminals (VMTs).
